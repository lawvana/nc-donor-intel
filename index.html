About This Dashboard

Data Source: NC State Board of Elections public campaign finance database at cf.ncsbe.gov, updated each business night.

How to Search:

  • Enter any NC judicial candidate's last name (e.g. "Dietz", "Riggs")
  • The tool finds their campaign committee(s) then pulls all contribution receipts
  • Default filter: $1,000+ donations. Adjust the Min Amount field as needed
  • Use the inline filter bar to narrow by donor name, city, or employer
  • Export to CSV for Excel analysis or PDF for print reports

Limitations: NCSBE records go back to 1989 but many older/county reports are paper-only and not in the searchable database. When live data isn't available, the dashboard shows representative sample data.

Legal Note: All data is public record per N.C.G.S. § 163-278.11. For research/due diligence only. Not legal advice.
