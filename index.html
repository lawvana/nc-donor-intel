How to Get Your Data

1. Go to cf.ncsbe.gov/CFTxnLkup

2. In the Committee field, type the judge's name. Set Type to Receipts. Leave Amount blank to get all donors.

3. Click Search. On the results page, click "Export data to .CSV".

4. Come back here, click Update Data, and upload that file. Done.

When to update: NCSBE updates overnight. New filing periods are: Jan, Apr, Jul, Oct, and pre/post election. Upload a fresh CSV after each one.

All data is public record per N.C.G.S. § 163-278.11
